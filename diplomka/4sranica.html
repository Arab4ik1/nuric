About Updates Glc

Fact 1

LEDs will already be standard, and for a surcharge, the GLC will be equipped with smart adaptive Multibeam headlights, which can only make you coffee. They illuminate turns and turn off part of the light beam so as not to blind other drivers, and adapt to weather conditions.

The second biggest change is new, also diode taillights. The bumpers and grille have also been barely noticeably changed. But if you did not fix it right away, do not reproach yourself: the alterations are really cosmetic.

Fact 2

For example, a new steering wheel appeared here - the same as in all modern Mercedes: with plump silver spokes and a pair of touch panels. Moreover, the touchpad, which is located on the left, is responsible for interacting with another GLC update - a digital dashboard.

The screen diagonal is 12.3 inches. There are three design options to choose from, the content can be changed at will. The drawing of details is excellent, the resolution is high, the processor is fast. Here, perhaps, is all you need to know about this shield.

But the most important thing is the new entertainment complex. The MBUX multimedia system got to the GLC instead of the old Comand, and even with additions that other Mercedes do not yet have. We are talking about an application store with which you can install, for example, music services, if those that live in a smartphone are bored to death.

Fact 3

Twin-scroll turbines and an EQ Boost starter generator were added to the gasoline engines. It is powered by the 48V onboard power system and provides a smoother start when Start/Stop is engaged and also adds 150Nm on acceleration to help the engine until it spins up to 2500rpm.

In the sport mode, the GLC invigorates, and everything would be fine, but when switching, the box starts to kick unpleasantly. I assume that this was a feature of the test machine. If so, then I would not turn off Sport in the GLC 300 if I were you. Just as it would not turn on Sport +: in it, the GLC wants too much to seem like what it is not - a whimsical lighter.

Fact 4

In addition to the already mentioned new multimedia and fully digital tidy, the GLC has also improved electronic assistants. So, for example, adaptive cruise control now not only knows how to keep a distance from the car in front and steer according to the markings, but also adapt to the current speed limits and slow down before turns on the route.

It is clear that all this is a step towards a bright unmanned future. Moreover, the GLC has also learned to communicate with road infrastructure using radio signals and, based on this data, warn the driver of a potential danger.
